Top hotels you must visit in Sri Lanka.

  1. Cinnamon Grand - Colombo.
  2. Lighthouse hotel - Galle.
  3. Tudor Barn, Little England Cottages - Nuwara Eliya.
  4. The Queens Hotel - Kandy.
  5. Shangri-La - Hambantota.

1. Cinnamon Grand - Colombo.

The Cinnamon Grand Colombo is a luxury hotel located in Colombo, the capital city
of Sri Lanka. The hotel offered a range of well-appointed rooms and suites, catering to
both business and leisure travelers. The rooms were equipped with modern amenities and
provided a comfortable stay.
Cinnamon Grand Colombo had several restaurants and bars offering a diverse selection of
cuisines, from local Sri Lankan dishes to international delicacies. Guests could enjoy a variety of dining experiences during their stay.
The hotel boasted a range of facilities, including a swimming pool, fitness center, spa, and
business center, to cater to the needs of guests seeking relaxation or conducting business.
The Cinnamon Grand Colombo also has extensive conference and event facilities, suitable for
hosting business conferences, weddings, and other social gatherings.

2. Lighthouse hotel - Galle.

The Lighthouse Hotel in Galle is a luxury beachfront property that offers stunning views of
the Indian Ocean.
It is situated close to the historical Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, which makes it an attractive destination for tourists exploring the area.
The hotel is known for its elegant architecture, upscale amenities, and top-notch services.
It typically provides a range of accommodation options, from well-appointed rooms to suites and private villas.
Many of the rooms boast ocean views, and guests can enjoy access to various facilities such as swimming pools, restaurants, bars, spa services, and more.

3. Tudor Barn, Little England Cottages - Nuwara Eliya.

Little England Cottages is one of the best luxurious hotels in Nuwara Eliya.
It is a luxury gated community complex with stunning cottages. The cottages has range of
facilities like Mountain views, heated floor, piped sounds, high comfortable rooms and more.

Each cottage is owned and managed by different individuals with a fully equipped club house- Pedro Barn Hotel- which offers an ala carte restaurant, well stocked sports pub, gym and
wellness center.

4. The Queens Hotel - Kandy.

The Queens is a historic hotel with colonial architecture. The hotel is situated in Kandy and it's hotel which one of the oldest hotels in Sri Lanka. The Queens Hotel was originally built as a
residence for British officers during the colonial periods.
Hotel's architecture reflects a colonial charm and grandeur, with elements of Victorian and
Edwardian styles.
The hotel provides a range of facilities to its guests, including restaurants serving both
local and international cuisine, a bar, conference rooms, and recreational areas.
And also, Queens hotel occasionally hosts cultural performances and events to showcase
Sri Lanka's traditional arts and entertainment.

5. Shangri-La - Hambantota.

Overlooking the pristine southern coast of beautiful Sri Lanka, Shangri-La Hambantota is
located along the ancient Spice Route in a city steeped in rich history. The luxury resort spans 58 hectares, making it the largest resort in Sri Lanka, and boasts 274 spacious rooms,
including 20 suites, all surrounded by stunning tropical gardens.
The most highlighted thing of this hotel is that you can play at the first and only resort golf
course in Sri Lanka, for a memorable golfing experience with spectacular views of the Indian Ocean.
Some of the highlited facilities of the hotel are, 18-hole golf course, fitness classes, 3 outdoor swimming pools, Conference rooms and super luxurious rooms.
